At Arabic Middle east, our mission is to increase the usage and understanding of the Arabic language on a global scale. We provide Quran and Arabic language courses to students all over the world. We pray to Allah that this journey of knowledge leads to paradise (Jannah). Our primary goal is to please Allah and His Prophet Mohammed (peace be upon him) by teaching the Quran and Arabic language. We take great care in the services we provide, and our teachers use unique teaching methods where students are taught solely using the Arabic language from day one. We use ongoing quizzes to monitor the progress of our students, in addition to final exams at the end of each level.
